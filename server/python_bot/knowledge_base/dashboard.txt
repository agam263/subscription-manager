SubManager - Dashboard (Home Page)
====================================

The Dashboard is the main page after logging in. It gives you a complete financial overview of your subscriptions.

URL: http://localhost:5173/ (after login)

WHAT YOU SEE ON THE DASHBOARD:

1. STAT CARDS (top row, 3 cards):
   - Monthly Spending: Total amount spent on subscriptions in the current calendar month.
   - Yearly Spending: Total amount spent on subscriptions in the current calendar year.
   - Active Subscriptions: Count of subscriptions currently marked as "active".
   All amounts are shown in your selected base currency (configured in Settings > Currency).

2. RECENTLY PAID (bottom left card):
   - Shows subscriptions that were last billed in the past 7 days.
   - Helps you see what you just paid for.
   - Includes subscription name, amount, and billing date.

3. UPCOMING RENEWALS (bottom center card):
   - Shows subscriptions due for renewal within the next 7 days.
   - Helps you anticipate upcoming charges.
   - Includes subscription name, amount, and how many days until renewal.

4. CATEGORY BREAKDOWN (bottom right card):
   - A pie/donut chart of spending grouped by category (e.g., Entertainment, Productivity, Cloud).
   - Visually shows which categories consume the most budget.

5. REFRESH BUTTON (top-right of the header area):
   - Clicking "Refresh" triggers manual renewal processing and refreshes all spending data.
   - Use this to update the dashboard after adding new subscriptions or changing billing dates.

6. ANIMATED BACKGROUND:
   - The dashboard has a flowing laser/particle animation in the background for visual flair.

HOW TO USE THE DASHBOARD:
- The dashboard loads automatically when you log in.
- Data updates automatically on page load.
- Use the Refresh button to force re-fetch spending data and process any due renewals.
- If the page is loading, you'll see skeleton placeholders while data fetches.

SPENDING DATA:
- Monthly and yearly spending figures are calculated server-side via the analytics API.
- They account for all active subscriptions and their billing cycles.
- Exchange rates are applied automatically to convert all amounts to your base currency.
